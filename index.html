About Me

Hey! Kavya here. I'm a Colombian music producer and developer with a deep passion for the craft. I've been part of the Black MIDI community for over 10 years — and most of my time still goes into making those.

Alongside that, I mod Windows ISOs and build custom toolkits tailored for embedded systems and personal rigs. It's mostly been a private affair, but I might start sharing those publicly right here — so stay tuned.

Music & Black MIDI

Music is a core part of what I do. I produce original tracks, explore the extreme end of MIDI composition known as Black MIDI, and experiment with SoundFonts, synthesis and audio engineering.

Windows Modding

It's been 12 years since I fell into the Windows modding rabbit hole — and I still can't get enough of it. My main focus is crafting serviceable Windows ISOs purpose-built for embedded systems, gaming consoles, and thin clients: lean, stable, and tuned for the hardware they run on.

As soon as I start making those public, you'll be able to find them in the Projects tab. Until then, consider this a teaser.
